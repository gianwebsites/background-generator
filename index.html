-About

Hello, my name is Gianluca. I have created this Background Color Generator to help anyone creating fun, eclectic, professional background colors for their websites.

By clicking on the orange button you will be able to play with different background color and decide which one is the best for your project. I have chosen 6 different options you can experiment with:

  • Linear gradient (to right)

    • will give you the option to choose 2 colors gradiencies from left to right

  • Diagonal gradient

    • will give you the option to choose 2 colors gradiencies from top/left to bottom/right

  • Linear gradient (to bottom)

    • by default it will give you the option to choose 2 colors gradiencies from top to bottom

  • circle gradient

    • it will give you the option to choose 2 colors circular gradiencies, from center to perifery

  • Radial gradient (ellips)

    • it will give you the option to choose 2 colors elliptical gradiencies, from center to perifery

  • Repeating-radial-gradient (ellips)

    • it will give you the option to choose 3 colors elliptical gradiencies, starting from center to perifery

After you choose the colors, a syntax will appear below the "Current Color Background" text, copy and past it into your CSS file and there you have it, the perfect gradient background for your website!
